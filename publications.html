Green Grocery is a MERN stack-based digital platform that connects farmers directly with vendors, eliminating middlemen and improving market access. It features multilingual support, voice input, real-time chat, and secure payments via Razorpay to enhance usability for rural users. The platform boosts digital engagement, simplifies agri-commerce, and supports inclusive growth in the agricultural sector.
